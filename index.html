About

With a love for storytelling, Whitney has nearly a decade in experience developing highly effective creative content for tech, lifestyle and design brands. After graduating from Parsons, School of Design, Michel went on to contribute on the editorial teams of Rolling Stone, GQ Style, Glamour, TIME & more. Her experience ranges from developing marketing & creative strategies, art direction, the ideation and production of visual campaigns (video/photo) to UI/UX design for tech startups using design programs in Adobe Creative Suite.
